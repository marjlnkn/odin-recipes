Beef Stew Slow Cooker

A beef stew that is tender and delicious.

  • Stewing Beef
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Beef Stock
  • Bayleaf
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Flour
  • Salt&pepper
  • Paprika
  1. Cut the potatoes, Carrots, Garlic(mince it) and onion into small chunks
  2. Sear the stewing beef, a few minutes on each side in a hot pan with some garlic
  3. Boil water and add beef stock, at least 2.5 cups will be needed.
  4. Add All the ingredients to a slow cooker,mix together and cook on high for 4 hrs or low for 8 hrs
  5. Once time has elapsed, add 1/2 cup of flour, stir well, and cook an additional 30 minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
